How CloudCarry Works

Simple, transparent, and built for trust

1

List Your Journey

2

Smart Matching

Senders select from approved item categories like documents, clothing, or books. Our platform suggests fair pricing automatically, and payment is held securely in escrow until successful delivery.

Meet in person for an open-box inspection before departure. No sealed packages, no surprises. Travel with the item using your existing journey ‚Äî zero extra carbon footprint.
